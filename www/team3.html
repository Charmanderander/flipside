Cherie Tan

Bachelor of Laws, Singapore Management University (SMU L.L.B)

Areas of expertise: English and Humanities

Subjects taught: English, Geography, History, Social Studies

Grades attained: A1 for English / Geography / Literature / Social Studies at GCE ‘O’ Levels, A for H1 General Paper / H2 Geography / H2 Literature at GCE ‘A’ Levels. Also awarded the Top Student in Arts Stream for her performance at the ‘A’ Levels

As a student, Cherie had always harboured a fervent passion for the English language and the Humanities. This passion has since translated into a love for teaching these subjects, and she is persistently dedicated to helping students cultivate a genuine interest and appreciation for the subjects before attempting to excel in them. Cherie also believes strongly in taking a skills-based approach rather than pure traditional rote learning, and seeks to transform her students into critical thinkers to give them an edge over their peers.

Cherie believes that in every student lies the potential for him or her to succeed — and therefore often dedicates her time to establishing a strong rapport and connecting with her students, as it is her firm belief that the key to effective learning is trust and support.
