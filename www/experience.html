There was a real struggle there, because while the intention was as such, Isaac felt that there was no clear guidance nor coaching in aiding students to become self-sufficient.

Isaac is now determined to help students avoid the unnecessary struggle that he has had to face growing up amidst the intense competition. Learning is a simple process - though not always easy. However, it helps to have a teacher like Isaac who will lighten up the atmosphere with his jokes that are occasionally funny. He tries his best.
